\subsection{Main Contributions}

In our work, we consider the predictive problem of regression, modeling the strategic interaction as a sender-receiver game. 

Assuming the environment is fully known to both players, we establish sufficient conditions in the regression setting for the predictive model to benefit from voluntary disclosure. These conditions depend on the misalignment between the sender's preferred value and the ground truth value. We also provide necessary conditions that depend on the relationship between the disclosed features and the ground truth.
