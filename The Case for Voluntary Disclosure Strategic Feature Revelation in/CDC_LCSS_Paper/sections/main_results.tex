\section{MAIN RESULTS}\label{sec:main_results}

In this section, we characterize the conditions under which a strategic advantage arises. Throughout our analysis, we adopt the shorthand notation $\yhatempt$ to denote the Receiver's prediction when features are withheld (also called default prediction), $\yhatx$ when features are revealed, $\yhatm$ when evaluating on the observed message $M$, and $\yhatvm$ for the vanilla predictor's output on $M$. 

We analyze the regression problem under the assumption of a known environment, deriving distinct necessary and sufficient conditions for the emergence of a strategic advantage. Detailed proofs for all results in this section are deferred to the Postponed Proofs section~\ref{sec:postponed_proofs}.
